Privacy Policy

Effective March 11, 2026

At TrainingOS, we believe your personal data belongs to you. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our fitness training application. We are committed to transparency and to safeguarding your privacy at every step.

01

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when creating an account, building workouts, and using TrainingOS features. This includes:

  • Account details such as name, email address, and profile preferences
  • Workout data including exercises, sets, reps, weight, and duration
  • YouTube URLs you submit for workout import functionality
  • Device information and usage analytics to improve app performance
  • Subscription and payment metadata processed through Apple or Google
02

How We Use Your Information

Your information powers the core TrainingOS experience. We use it to deliver personalized workout recommendations through our AI coaching engine, to track your fitness progress over time, and to continuously improve our service.

Specifically, we use your data to operate and maintain your account, generate AI-driven training insights and suggestions, synchronize your workouts across devices, communicate important service updates, and analyze aggregate usage patterns to improve TrainingOS.

03

Third-Party Services

TrainingOS integrates with select third-party services to deliver its core functionality. These include the YouTube Data API for workout video analysis, cloud infrastructure providers for secure data storage, analytics services for app performance monitoring, and payment processors for subscription management.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Each third-party service we use is bound by its own privacy policy and our contractual data protection agreements.

04

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on encrypted servers with industry-standard security measures. We employ TLS encryption for all data in transit, AES-256 encryption for data at rest, regular security audits and penetration testing, and strict access controls with multi-factor authentication for all internal systems.

While no system is perfectly secure, we implement commercially reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

05

YouTube Content

When you import workouts from YouTube, TrainingOS accesses video metadata through the YouTube Data API Services. We process video titles, descriptions, and timestamps to extract workout structure. We do not download, store, or redistribute any YouTube video content.

Your use of YouTube import features is also subject to Google's Privacy Policy and YouTube's Terms of Service. You may revoke TrainingOS's access to your YouTube data at any time through your Google security settings.

06

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Depending on your jurisdiction, you may also have the right to data portability, the right to restrict or object to certain processing, and the right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@trainingos.app. We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.

07

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. Workout history and training data are retained to enable long-term progress tracking and AI coaching improvements.

If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data from our active systems within 30 days. Some data may be retained in encrypted backups for up to 90 days before permanent deletion. Anonymized, aggregate data may be retained indefinitely for analytical purposes.

08

Children's Privacy

TrainingOS is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will take immediate steps to delete that information from our servers.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@trainingos.app.

09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or via email before the changes take effect.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of TrainingOS after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

10

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or our data practices, please reach out to us. We take every inquiry seriously and are committed to resolving any issues promptly.

Email: privacy@trainingos.app

For general support inquiries, visit our Support page.
