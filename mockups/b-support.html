Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about TrainingOS.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Since subscriptions are managed through Apple, you can cancel anytime by going to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone, then selecting TrainingOS and tapping "Cancel Subscription." Your access will continue until the end of the current billing period.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, and select the TrainingOS purchase. Apple typically processes refund requests within 48 hours.

Is there a free tier?

TrainingOS does not currently offer a free tier. However, both subscription plans include a 7-day free trial so you can explore all features before being charged. You can cancel during the trial period at no cost.

How does the YouTube import work?

Simply paste a YouTube workout video URL into TrainingOS. Our AI analyzes the video's metadata and description to extract the workout structure -- exercises, sets, reps, and timing. It then creates a structured, trackable program you can follow and log. We don't download or store any video content.

How is my data kept secure?

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols. We use SOC 2 compliant cloud infrastructure and never sell your personal information. Your workout data is only used to power your experience within TrainingOS. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from within the app by going to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your personal data, workout history, and programs within 30 days. Alternatively, email support@trainingos.app and we'll process it for you.

The app keeps crashing. What should I do?

First, make sure you're running the latest version of TrainingOS from the App Store. If the issue persists, try restarting your device. If crashes continue, please email us at support@trainingos.app with your device model and iOS version so we can investigate the issue.

I'm having issues with my subscription. Help!

If your subscription isn't being recognized, try restoring purchases from Settings > Account > Restore Purchases in the app. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. If the issue continues, check your subscription status in iOS Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions, or contact us at support@trainingos.app.
