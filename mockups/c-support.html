FAQ

Frequently asked questions

01

How do I cancel my subscription?

Since TrainingOS uses Apple's In-App Purchase system, subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, go to Subscriptions, find TrainingOS, and tap Cancel Subscription. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

02

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are processed by Apple, not directly by TrainingOS. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find your TrainingOS purchase, and submit a refund request. Apple typically processes these within 48 hours.

03

Is there a free tier?

TrainingOS offers a limited free experience that lets you explore the app and create basic workouts. To unlock the full suite of features including AI coaching, YouTube imports, advanced analytics, and unlimited workout storage, a subscription is required. We offer a free trial so you can experience everything before committing.

04

How does YouTube import work?

Simply copy the URL of any YouTube workout video and paste it into TrainingOS. Our AI analyzes the video metadata and content to extract exercises, sets, reps, and rest periods, then generates a fully structured, trackable workout. You can edit any details before saving it to your library.

05

How secure is my data?

We take data security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols. We use Firebase for secure authentication and storage. We never sell your personal information. For full details, please review our Privacy Policy.

06

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from within the app by navigating to Settings and selecting "Delete Account." This will permanently remove all your personal data, workout history, and account information within 90 days. Please cancel your subscription first to avoid further charges. You can also email us at support@trainingos.app to request deletion.

07

The app keeps crashing. What should I do?

First, make sure you are running the latest version of TrainingOS from the App Store. Try force-quitting the app and restarting it. If the issue persists, restart your device. You can also try uninstalling and reinstalling the app. If none of these steps help, please contact us with your device model and iOS version.

08

I'm having issues with my subscription.

If your subscription status is not reflecting correctly, first check your subscription status in iPhone Settings under your Apple ID. Make sure the payment method on file is valid. Try restoring purchases from the app settings. If the problem continues, contact us at support@trainingos.app with a screenshot of your subscription status and we will help resolve it.
