Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription? +

Subscriptions are managed through your device's app store. On iOS, go to Settings, tap your name, then Subscriptions, find TrainingOS and tap Cancel. On Android, open the Google Play Store, go to Subscriptions, select TrainingOS and tap Cancel. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

Can I get a refund? +

Refund requests are handled by Apple or Google, depending on your platform. You can request a refund through Apple's Report a Problem page or through Google Play's order history. If you need further assistance, contact us at support@trainingos.app and we will do our best to help.

Is there a free tier? +

TrainingOS offers a limited free experience that lets you explore the app and try basic workout tracking. The full feature set, including AI coaching, unlimited YouTube imports, cloud sync, and advanced analytics, requires an active subscription at $5.99 per month or $49.99 per year.

How does YouTube import work? +

Paste any YouTube workout video URL into TrainingOS. Our system uses the YouTube Data API to analyze the video's metadata, title, description, and timestamps. It then extracts the workout structure, including exercises, sets, reps, and rest periods, creating a fully trackable workout you can save, modify, and repeat. We never download or store the video itself.

How is my data kept secure? +

We use TLS encryption for all data in transit and AES-256 encryption for data at rest. Our infrastructure is hosted on leading cloud providers with SOC 2 compliance. We conduct regular security audits, enforce strict internal access controls, and never sell your personal data. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my account? +

To delete your account, go to Settings within the TrainingOS app and select "Delete Account." This will permanently remove all your personal data, workout history, and preferences within 30 days. Encrypted backups are purged within 90 days. You can also request deletion by emailing privacy@trainingos.app. Please cancel your subscription separately through your app store before deleting your account.

The app keeps crashing. What should I do? +

First, make sure you are running the latest version of TrainingOS from your app store. Try force-quitting the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, restart your device. Clearing the app cache in your device settings can also help. If none of these steps resolve the problem, please email us at support@trainingos.app with your device model, OS version, and a brief description of when the crash occurs.

My subscription is not being recognized. How do I fix it? +

First, try restoring your purchases within the app by going to Settings and tapping "Restore Purchases." Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account you used to subscribe. If the issue continues, check your subscription status in your device's app store settings. As a last resort, contact us at support@trainingos.app with your receipt or transaction ID and we will resolve it promptly.
